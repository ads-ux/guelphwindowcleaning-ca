How Much Does Window Cleaning Cost in Guelph?

Published 2026-06-01 — Guelph Window Cleaning

Professional window cleaning in Guelph is priced per window, per pane, or as a flat rate for the home. Here's what to expect in 2026.

Typical Price Ranges

  • Exterior only — bungalow or single storey: $120–$200
  • Exterior only — two storey home: $180–$300
  • Interior + exterior — bungalow: $200–$320
  • Interior + exterior — two storey: $280–$450
  • Per window (both panes): $8–$18 depending on size and accessibility
  • Skylights: $20–$40 each
  • Storm windows (removable panels): Add $3–$6 per panel

What's Included in a Standard Cleaning

A professional window cleaning in Guelph typically includes:

  • Washing both panes of each window
  • Cleaning window frames and sills
  • Squeegee-drying to a streak-free finish
  • Wiping down interior sill with a cloth

Screen cleaning is often an add-on ($1–$3 per screen). Tracks and sill debris removal beyond standard wiping may also be additional.

What Drives the Price Up

  • Accessibility: Windows above a covered porch, setback from the roofline, or requiring ladder positioning on uneven ground take longer
  • Window condition: Hard water stains, paint overspray, or construction residue require chemical treatment beyond standard cleaning
  • Window count: Homes with many small windows (Victorian or older Guelph homes downtown) take longer than a home with fewer, larger panes
  • Third storey: Requires specialized equipment or water-fed pole systems at additional cost

Getting an Accurate Quote

The most accurate quotes come from a technician counting your windows directly. For phone quotes, have ready: your home's approximate age (older homes tend to have more, smaller windows), number of storeys, and whether any windows are in hard-to-access locations. Interior cleaning takes significantly more time than exterior — about 40% of the total job time — so clarifying whether you want interior + exterior upfront ensures accurate pricing.
